# BDB Pain Log

---

## Pain 001

### Problem

At James Caterers, when the internet goes down, Shireburn cannot be used.

Invoices cannot be created.

Business stops.

### Why is this bad?

A business should never stop because the internet stopped.

### BDB Solution

Core business information should remain available locally.

When internet returns, the system synchronises automatically.

If conflicts occur, they are presented to the user through a Conflict Resolution Centre.

The AI may recommend a solution, but a human must approve the final decision.
