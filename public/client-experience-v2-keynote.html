04 · Role-aware design

Owner and Staff are genuinely different products.

Owner

  • Accounts, banking and reports
  • Team and role management
  • Financial quick actions
  • Management-level attention items
  • Full client workspace, excluding Founder Admin

Staff

  • Customers, calendar and communications
  • Documents needed for daily work
  • Appointment and follow-up actions
  • No financial leakage
  • No disabled management clutter
Responsibility, not greyed-out temptation05
05 · Premium interaction

Small details now explain state.

Spatial continuity

View transitions, drawers and modal flows preserve context instead of abruptly replacing the screen.

Tactile feedback

Pressed states, completion feedback, animated sync and success toasts confirm what happened.

Progressive disclosure

Command search, quick create, notifications and context drawers keep the main canvas calm.

Business Pulse

The circular BDB motif communicates workload and identity without becoming redundant navigation.

Responsive mobile

Mobile prioritises daily work with five tabs rather than shrinking the desktop hierarchy.

Reduced motion

Non-essential animation respects the user’s system preference.

Premium without theatrical clutter06
06 · Offline-first direction

Offline behaviour is visible and honest.

2

Queued changes

The interface clearly states what will sync when the connection returns.

Core records remain available

Customers, today’s appointments, recent messages and selected documents are represented as local-capable.

No false promises

The prototype does not claim that persistence, conflicts or bank connections are already engineered.

A badge can explain offline state. It cannot implement distributed systems through optimism.
Offline-first reliability07
07 · Harsh review verdict

This now qualifies as a serious client product direction.

Approved for founder review

The complete interactive concept is coherent, premium, customer-centred and practical enough to test.

Not approved for direct merge

Static data and local state prove interaction, not production reliability.

What must be challenged

Task priority, business language, role boundaries, mobile density and financial trust.
