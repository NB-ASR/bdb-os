04 · Offline-first

Offline is a workflow, not a badge.

1Go offlineCore customer and operational records remain visible.
2Keep workingNotes, messages and record updates save locally.
3Queue clearlyThe interface counts pending changes and pauses cloud services.
4ReconnectA visible sync state clears the queue.

AI pauses honestly

Cloud suggestions stop offline instead of pretending to work.

External feeds pause

Banking and billing updates wait while existing records remain readable.

Engineering remains

Real persistence, retries and conflict resolution still require production work.

08 · What remains

A polished prototype is not production infrastructure.

Production engineering

Validated workspace themes, server-side roles, real data/storage and verified billing and banking actions.

Offline reliability

Local database, retry strategy, sync ordering, conflicts, audit history and recovery states.

Quality proof

Formal WCAG testing, 200% zoom, long datasets, low-powered devices, real users and feature flags.
